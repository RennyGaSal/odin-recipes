Delicious cream of broccoli soup

This broccoli soup recipe is thick and flavorful. It is simple, delicious, and quick to make. Enjoy (I know you will).

Make restaurant-worthy cream of broccoli soup in the comfort of your own kitchen with this top-rated recipe.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Onions and celery
  • Broth
  • Broccoli
  • Flour, milk and butter
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the vegetables
  2. Blend the ingredients
  3. Thicken the soup
